A look at Eagles Uniforms over the years.


Notable Dates:

1933: Philadelphia Eagles franchise founded by Bert Bell and Lud Wray.

1945: After several early versions, Kelly Green becomes the primary uniform color.

1948: Eagles win first of two consective NFL championships.

1954: Eagles become the first franchise to put team logo on helmets, which are Kelly Green.

1960: Eagles win third and Final NFL championship, defeating the Green Bay Packers.

1980: In first Super Bowl appearance, Eagles lose to the Oakland Raiders.

1994: Jeff Lurie becomes owner of the Philadelphia Eagles.

1996: Lurie changes primary team color from Kelly to Midnight Green.

2015: "Bring Back the Kelly Green" organization is formed.
